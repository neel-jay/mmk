# Ganeshotsav 2024 Celebrations in Krakow

The Maharashtra Mandal Krakow celebrated Ganeshotsav 2024 with great enthusiasm and devotion at the Radha Govind Bhakti Yog Temple in Krakow. The 10-day festival brought together the Marathi community and friends from various Indian communities in Poland.

## Event Highlights

- **Ganpati Sthapana**: The idol of Lord Ganesha was installed with traditional rituals on the first day (Anga Choth)
- **Daily Aarti**: Morning and evening aartis performed by the community
- **Cultural Programs**: Various cultural performances including traditional Marathi dances, songs, and skits
- **Visarjan**: The grand procession and immersion ceremony on the final day

## Community Participation

The event saw overwhelming participation from the community members. Families came together to celebrate, bringing traditional sweets and offerings. The temple was beautifully decorated with flowers and lights throughout the festival.
